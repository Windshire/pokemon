Pokemon Strength Rater

This webpage will be a tool for more accurately assessing the battling strength of a Pokemon in Pokemon GO, because CP absolutely sucks as an indicator.

Show/Hide Development Progress Notes and Comments

Right now, I'm just trying to get a basic idea of how to program behavior into webpages. Once I get closer to actually creating something useful, then I will start to look at making the UI not look like trash. But that is not a priority right now. I just want to learn about functionality.

June 1 2017: On this first session, I was able to code the algorithms used to compute attack damage. Since I focused on this, I haven't looked into actually selecting specific Pokemon and obtaining unique data for them. So every Pokemon is a Vaporeon with 0% IVs and a Water Gun/Hydro Pump moveset. The calculations, however, work correctly, and will continue to work correctly once I begin to plug other types of Pokemon and moves in.

June 26 2017: Came back and put in stuff where you can select different kinds of Pokemon, and it goes and figures out what moves they can learn, and the result is different depending on which Pokemon and moves you select. It grabs the info from a JSON object, but I haven't finished that GAME_MASTER to JSON parser thing, yet. So far I only manually put in stuff for the Gen 1 starters into the existing data thingey. I also disabled calculation of type effectiveness for now. And I didn't add anything extra, so it still only tells you how much damage you do to the defender, and not vice versa.
